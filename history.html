The Humble Beginnings

The Department of Physics at Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University was founded in 2010 under the Faculty of Science during the tenure of Vice-Chancellor Professor Dr. M. Nurul Islam, with Professor Dr. Md. Anwar Hossain serving as the 1st Chairman.

Starting with a single faculty member, Professor Dr. Md. Anwar Hossain, and 25 undergraduate students, the Department of Physics began its academic journey in the old academic building on February 6, 2011. Soon after, Professors Dr. Md. Moniruzzaman and Dr. Masum Haider joined the department, further strengthening its foundation. Despite limited resources, the journey was driven by boundless dreams and a shared commitment to progress. The early years brought many challenges, but the devotion and hard work of the faculty, along with the unity among students and teachers, laid the groundwork for the vibrant department we see today.

This history walk honors those formative years and the people whose passion and perseverance made it all possible.

The Founding Faculty

These pioneering educators laid the foundation for academic excellence in our department

Dr. Md. Anwar Hossain

Professor & 1st Chairman • Joined: 2008

Directed the department during its formative years, establishing curriculum standards, academic policies, a strong faculty team, and foundational infrastructure, while initiating research initiatives that continue to guide our academic endeavors.

Dr. Md. Masum Haider

Professor & 2nd Chairman • Joined: 2011

Advanced student development through diverse academic and extracurricular initiatives, and accelerated the expansion of departmental infrastructure, facilities, and resources.

Dr. Md. Moniruzzaman

Professor & 3rd Chairman • Joined: 2011

In the early days, mentored students and fostered their philosophical development, transforming the limitations of minimal lab resources into opportunities to practice creative imagination and independent scientific reasoning.

Department Timeline

2010 - Department Establishment

Founding Year

Department of Physics officially established under faculty of Science.

2011 - Under Graduate Admission

1st Batch

Department of Physics officially established with initial intake of 25 students and 3 faculty members in the old academic building.

2015 - MSc Program Launched

Graduate Studies

Master of Science program in Physics launched.

2016 - First Graduating Batch

Milestone

19 students from the inaugural batch graduate, beginning the department's alumni legacy.
